The ai act is an proposition of the european laws proposed the february 21th 2021 to limit risks of ai, by rating those risks (low, limited, high and unacceptable)

Unacceptable risk (they are in general the risk which harm to UE values and fundamental rights) :

  • social rating of citizens with facial recognition in public space
  • keep a record on person because of their political ideas, religious, etc...
  • emotion recognition at work

High risks (that affect safety or fundamental rights) :

  • Some AI will need to be registered in a database to use it, to make sure that's in safe, like in these work sections :
    • Education
    • Employment
    • Law enforcement
    • Assistance in legal interpretation

There's an other type of AI, the generative ones, like Chat GPT which will have to respect these requirements :

  • Tell that the content is generated by AI
  • Protect the images created to prevent it from illegal content
  • Publish a text with all the data used for training ai
